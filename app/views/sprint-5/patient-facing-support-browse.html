Patient-facing support

There are plenty of ways you can make patients lives a little more comfortable. You’ll read together, chat, play games, take a walk or help them keep in contact with their families. If you are someone who enjoys direct contact with people, these roles are for you.

Some of the roles might include:

  • Patient companion or visitor
  • Mealtime support volunteer
  • Pets as therapy support volunteer
  • Refreshment trolley volunteer
