Role summary

This is a rewarding volunteering opportunity within our mental health services for people with memory problems or dementia and their carers. You will support our therapy team with the running of this activity with the aim of maintaining and improving their well-being.

This role will suits someone friendly, approachable and enthusiastic. Whether you want to gain experience, improve your career prospects or just want to give some of your time to help others, this role could be for you!


Main duties of the role
  • To encourage and support service users to participate in this therapeutic and social activity
  • To meet and greet service users as they arrive
  • To encourage people to participate and help them to feel good about themselves
  • To direct walkers along the designated route
