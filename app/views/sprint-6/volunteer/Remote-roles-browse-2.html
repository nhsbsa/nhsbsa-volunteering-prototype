Remote opportunities

Volunteering is changing its face as times change. You can now offer your support to patients remotely. Bright up someone's day with just a 30min phone call or a video chat. You can check up on them, make sure they have taken their medicine or just talk about the weather with a cup of tea. Your time and dedication will go a long way towards reducing the feeling of isolation and loneliness.
