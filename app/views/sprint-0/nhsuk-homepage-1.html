Introduction

Welcome and thank you for your interest in volunteering with the NHS in England.

Volunteering with the NHS is an excellent opportunity to help your own community. It is a rewarding way to dedicate your time to a cause you care about.

You can use your life, work experience and knowledge to support patients and staff. Volunteering with the NHS can also give you the skills you need in your own life and career.
