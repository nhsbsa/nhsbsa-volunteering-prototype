Patient-facing support

There are plenty of ways you can make patients' lives a little more comfortable. You’ll read together, chat, play games, take a walk or help them keep in contact with their families. If you are someone who enjoys direct contact with people, these roles are for you.



Walk and talk volunteer, Pennine Care NHS Trust

  • Main responsibilities: help run a walking group for people with memory problems or dementia, support service users to participate in social activities
  • Three hours per week, (9am-7pm), Tuesday or Thursday
  • CMHT Older People, Stockport SK2 5EQ
Sign in and save role

Lived experience practitioner, Pennine Care NHS Trust

  • Main responsibilities: use your own experience with mental health conditions to help others in crisis
  • Flexible hours, Monday to Wednesday
  • Memorial Hospital, Shooters Hill, London SE18 3RG
Sign in and save role

Community first responder (CFR), East of England Ambulance Service

  • Main responsibilities: administer emergency care, including patient assessment skills, cardiopulmonary resuscitation and the use of an automatic external defibrillator (AED).
  • Eight hours per week, (8am-12pm/1pm-5pm), Monday to Sunday
  • Various areas in East England, view the exact locations on role listing
