Remote opportunities

Volunteering is changing its face as times change. You can now offer your support to patients remotely. Bright up someone's day with just a 30min phone call or a video chat. You can check up on them, make sure they have taken their medicine or just talk about the weather with a cup of tea. Your time and dedication will go a long way towards reducing the feeling of isolation and loneliness.



Calls with care volunteer, England-wide

  • Main responsibilities: offer companionship and good conversation to isolated people, improve their mental health
  • Flexible hours, Monday to Sunday, Home-based
  • Basic phone competence needed, volunteers will use their own phone

Telephone befriender, England-wide

  • Main responsibilities: provide additional support for lone people, giving comfort, company and time.
  • 2 hours per week, flexible hours, Monday to Sunday, home-based.
  • Home-based. Basic phone or video conference needed (MS Teams or Google meet), volunteers will use their own phone.

Hospice call befriender, England-wide

  • Main responsibilities: offer reassurance, compassion and social interaction to people on the phone.
  • 3 hours per week, 2pm - 5pm, Monday to Friday, home-based.
