Introduction

Welcome and thank you for your interest in volunteering with the NHS in England.

Volunteering with the NHS is an excellent opportunity to help your own community. It is a rewarding way to dedicate your time to a cause you care about.

You can use your life, work experience and knowledge to support patients and staff. Volunteering with the NHS can also give you the skills you need in your own life and career.

What’s it like to be an NHS Volunteer?

As an NHS volunteer, you get to make a difference on the experience of patients and their families. Medical staff also very much value the contribution that volunteers can make to the delivery of high-quality care.

You will you be doing something really worthwhile, whilst meeting new people and gaining valuable experience.

Volunteering with the NHS is more than complementing the work of staff and providing a friendly and personal atmosphere. It can improve your job prospects and help you increase your confidence.
